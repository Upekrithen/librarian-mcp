## The Scene

On approximately **April 17, 2026**, the Founder walked into **Pine Books** at **Pike Place Market in Seattle** looking for a book his daughter wanted. He had **two words** left in his head of its seven-word title.

**Tiffany Brost** — the sixth caretaker / owner of Pine Books — listened to those two words, walked directly to the shelf, pulled the book, and named **three adjacent authors** his daughter might also like.

No search. No database. No browse. Two words in; book + three recommendations out.

## Why this scene matters

This IS the Librarian metaphor. Canonical memory + expertise + fast delivery — the concrete human analog of what R9 does for an AI model.
