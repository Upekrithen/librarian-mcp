# Rhetorical Keystones — Founder Voice Anchor Registry

**What this is:** Named Founder voice phrases that must survive every rewrite. A draft that strips a Keystone has drifted, no matter how clean the prose reads.

**Ratification rule:** Additions require Founder confirmation ("I wrote it, it stays"). This registry is authoritative through B111.

---

## Ratified Keystones (as of B111)

1. **"Every AI company is currently paying a tax they don't know they're paying."**
   Root-cause-diagnosis opener. B103 op-ed.

2. **"Especially from friendly fire."**
   The moral-weight anchor for any ask — speaker is one who has already taken the hit. Crown Letter to MacKenzie Scott (Cardboard Boots).

3. **"I pray for potatoes at the end of a hoe handle."**
   Humility-of-ask anchor. Concrete, rural, un-fancy. Cardboard Boots, Feb 2, 2026.

4. **"And I have two suits."**
   Un-fancy credibility anchor. Refuses false equivalence with resourced peers. Cardboard Boots closing.

5. **"I know enough to know I don't know enough."**
   Epistemic-humility anchor. Makes correction welcome, not threatening.

6. **"Nothing about us without us."**
   Governance-principle anchor. Commons designed by the people who use it.

7. **"The eighty percent is the only number where cooperation costs less than defection."**
   Math-of-cooperation anchor. B103 op-ed on patent strategy.

8. **"What we need is people and leadership; the money will follow."**
   Governance / economic-inversion anchor. Institutional design precedes funding. Across 8+ Crown Letter versions verbatim.

9. **"No Plan Survives First Contact."**
   Biographical / governance anchor. Licenses asking for help without weakness. Newmark LOCKED02 — the only all-caps moment.

10. **"The medallions are minted. The platform is built. The first ten members are ready. What I lack is the wisdom and experienced leadership to ensure we serve millions, not hundreds."**
    Catalytic closure stating completion-state and gap simultaneously. Scott + Seibel Crown Letters (6+ iterations verbatim).

11. **"Help each other help ourselves."**
    Philosophical / governance anchor. Mutual-aid inversion of charity framing. Seibel + Simon + Newmark closings.

12. **"I read a lot, and I am good at chess."**
    Biographical anchor. Understated credibility. Across Scott + multiple Crown Letters.

13. **"The way I learned things affected WHETHER I learned them."**
    Pedagogical / design-method anchor. Axiomatic form of the Anachronism Principle. Flips "what I learned" to "whether I learned at all" — the conditional that explains Founder's cross-domain fluency.

14. **"A rising tide lifts all boats. And I think I've built a system of wells."**
    Economic-sovereignty anchor. Classic Inversion Principle move: take the familiar "rising tide" cliché and one-up with structural inversion. Tides need a moon; wells need labor.

15. **"53 years of surviving the trenches of poordom, and I'm really good at it."**
    Biographical / competence anchor. Self-mockery + competence claim in one sentence. Pairs with Keystone #4 ("I have two suits") — #15 sets up the moral claim, #4 closes it.

16. **"A tool that measures its own value and shows only you, unless you agree to share it anonymously, or publicly."**
    Anti-enshittification / anti-opacity anchor. Three-tier user agency (private default / anonymous opt-in / public opt-in). Preserves the Doctorow-register sharpness while working cross-letter.

## How to apply

- Any draft / rewrite / letter / paper / op-ed / Spoonful / BST Episode / social post that removes or waters a Keystone = drift. Flag it.
- Scaffold drafts should mark Keystone placement explicitly for Founder voice pass.
- Registry growth: new candidates surface during SP-16 runs, transcript scans, or letter review. Propose to Founder with provenance + source. Founder ratifies or rejects.

---

## Why Keystones matter

- **Paired Provenance** (B109 pattern): every economic rule has an ethical/biographical twin. Keystones ARE those ethical/biographical twins.
- **Anti-drift mechanism:** historical "clean-up" passes have stripped Keystones — prose improves superficially while center-of-gravity rotates away from Founder voice.
