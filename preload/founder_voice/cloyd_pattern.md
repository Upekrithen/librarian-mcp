# The Cloyd Pattern — Pre-Extended Trust Repaid Through Demonstrated Labor

**What this is:** Named structural pattern. A party with reputation-assessment capability **extends credit or resource to a newcomer before the newcomer has earned it**, trusting the recipient to repay through **demonstrated labor** rather than collateral, contract, or upfront payment.

Surfaced by SP-16 Creative Recombiner on 2026-04-20 04:08. Four artifacts share the pattern; the pattern itself was never named until B110.

**Founder ratification (B110, April 20, 2026):**
> *"Yes, love it. Mr. Cloyd would be proud. And if he or his children are around, I will compensate them for that early confidence."*

## Instantiations (four known, more likely to surface)

1. **Mr. Cloyd / Sears Lewistown layaway** — biographical origin.
   Mr. Cloyd extended layaway / credit to the Founder's family at Sears Lewistown before any credit history justified it. The trust was repaid through the labor of working toward the purchase. This is the pattern's origin in Founder's biography.

2. **Courtesy SSL** — $50 no-questions-asked, platform mechanism.
   The platform extends $50 in structural credit to any member, no application, no questions. Repaid through the member's subsequent participation and demonstrated good-faith labor on the platform.

3. **Self-Sustaining Subsistence Subscription (SSSS)** — voucher-subscription product with a 3-month Marks runway.
   The platform extends operating credit before the member has earned it; the member works toward self-sustainability over the runway period.

4. **Destination Housing / Realtor cold-start.**
   Realtors receive Pedestal-equity position and Steward training on entry, before they have brokered a single cooperative transaction. They earn the position forward through demonstrated realtor labor within the cooperative framework.

---

## Why naming the pattern matters

- **Product design clarity.** SSSS and Destination Housing stop looking like two inventions and start looking like two deployments of one pattern. Reduces design surface; increases transfer.

- **Counsel framing.** The Cloyd Pattern is legally distinguishable from both charitable extension (no repayment expectation) and consumer credit (no Truth-in-Lending triggers, because it's labor-repayable, not dollar-repayable at interest). Counsel can reason about one pattern instead of four cases.

- **Crown Jewel candidacy.** Pattern-level innovation, not instantiation-level. Potentially its own formal innovation entry / Crown Jewel claim.

- **Cold-start messaging.** Every cooperative onboarding touch can be framed as "Cloyd extended" — names the social contract cleanly. Audiences who know a Cloyd figure from their own life recognize it instantly.
